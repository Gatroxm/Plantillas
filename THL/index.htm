Who we are

In this new era of digital media, new methods of marketing are needed to evoke responses from consumers. The Hidden London is a UK based marketing, talent and creative production agency with a new gen approach to digital marketing campaigns.

We provide clients with the strategies that help to connect brands to culture. By aiding in influencer selection, and providing creative production, we offer a full in-house service. Our aim is to help brands run successful campaigns across numerous platforms and to create long lasting partnerships.

What we focus on
Creativity, Connection & Culture

Creativity

We stay innovative by keeping creative and forward thinking. Always remaining ‘ahead of the curb’ to influence the latest trends and iconic fashion statements. Curating sets from the ground up, and having the best team of photographers and videographers on board means that brands have one less thing to worry about, as we have all the necessary tools needed to execute a vision, whether big or small - the possibilities are endless.

Connection

Focusing on representation and building connections individually and as partnerships is our number one priority as an agency. To create harmonious relationships ensures the best results for successful campaigns and enables longevity investments.

We have established relationships with a network of pioneering talents within an influential culture. This has given us a competitive advantage to create strategic partnerships. We aim to bridge the gap between brands and the culture, creating new sustainable connections.

Culture

Having an anthropologist amongst our team ensures a greater understanding in the importance of culture and representation, making sure all cultures feel enriched and not exploited through our marketing and featured campaigns.

We take a holistic approach by combining measurable data with culturally informed human insights to ensure we can communicate better with the target audience.
