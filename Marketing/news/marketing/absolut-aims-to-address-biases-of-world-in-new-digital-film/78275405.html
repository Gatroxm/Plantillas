          Absolut aims to address biases of world in new digital film

          The brand aims to challenge stereotypes, tackle biases and create a world with equal opportunities for all, through the film...

          • ETBrandEquity
          • Updated: September 23, 2020, 18:38 IST
          Vodka brand Absolut has launched a new digital film called ISMs, with an aim to address the biases that exist in the world and in continuation of the Born Colourless campaign.

          The company said the film is a testament to the brands philosophy, encouraging people to express themselves freely.

          The objective of 'ISMs', an idea conceptualised by Lowe Lintas, is to continue the effort for a colourless world through thought-provoking and genuine voices that question the status quo. Through the film, the brand aims to challenge stereotypes, tackle biases and create a world with equal opportunities for all.

          Read Also: IPL 2020: Digital ad spends to go up by atleast 20 percent this year

          ISMs highlights the stereotypes that are deeply entrenched in society to ultimately create a better world. It is emblazoned with biased terms around the LGBTQIA+ community, race, ethnicity, caste and more, covering both current local and global issues.
