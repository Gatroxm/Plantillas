Vegas Nay Lashes

We're so pleased to stock Vegas Nay lashes! We just can't get enough of these fab falsies, a collaboration between ones of the beauty industry's most popular Instagram-mers and world renowned lash brand, Eylure. Social media star Vegas Nay (real name Naomi Giannopoulos). Giannopoulos shot to fame thanks to her YouTube videos in which she showcases her 20 years’ experience as a professional makeup artist, through beauty related tips and advice. Since Vegas Nay was a child, she took an interest in art, makeup and fashion; she went on to be awarded a degree in Art Studio as well as a Master's degree in Fashion. It's obvious that Vegas Nay has a huge passion for the beauty industry, and likes to keep up to date with current trends. She began blogging as a hobby and soon became an online sensation with over two million followers from all over the world on popular platform Instagram.

Studying at one of the most prestigious fashion houses, as well as attending the Polimoda Fashion School in Florence Italy, where she was trained by Roberto Cavalli’s design team clearly reflects her obvious love for beauty and fashion. Her passion, as well as experience within the industry as one of the world’s most famous makeup artists, makes her collaboration with respected eyelash brand Eylure extremely exciting.

We stock the full range of Vegas Nay Lashes by Eylure, consisting of four incredible styles. Handmade using high quality hair, these falsies are certainly something special. With a style to suit all occasions, ranging from natural style 'Easy Elegance' to dramatic style 'Grand Glamor', there is definitely a design for you in this fantastic collection. Each brightly coloured box contains a pair of stunning lashes, along with a small container of special waterproof adhesive in order to ensure all your lash applications are perfect. We also stock individual lashes from the Vegas Nay range which comes complete with eyelash adhesive and remover, for those that prefer to create their own look; this product contains everything you need.

Treat yourself now and try a pair of these stunning lashes designed by trusted makeup professional, and celebrated social media personality – Vegas Nay. You will certainly be impressed!
