Travis Scott x McDonald’s Collection Finally Revealed

Posted On September 9, 2020 Geoff Desreumaux 0

  • 31shares

Travis Scott and McDonald’s revealed an entire apparel collection that also includes accessories like a giant Nugget body pillow.

McDonald’s introduced the Travis Scott meal yesterday and most of us were disappointed. The “Cactus Jack” meal was nothing really special: a Quarter Pounder with cheese topped with lettuce and bacon, a medium side of fires with BBQ sauce, and a Sprite with extra ice. This $6 combo represents what the American Rapper would always order when visiting the golden arches as a kid.

Related | The McDonald’s Travis Scott Meal Is Here

I was disappointed too. No merch, no toys, not even a limited-edition packaging, nothing. But all that changed today When Travis Scott finally shared the first merch drop issued from this collab. And, it is something very special indeed.

View this post on Instagram

Billions and billions served

A post shared by flame (@travisscott) on Sep 8, 2020 at 4:57pm PDT

The merch line features t-shirts, hoodies, sweatpants, shorts, boxers, embroidered print denim, polos, a burger tie, a work jacket, and a full McDonald’s/ Cactus Jack-branded basketball uniform complete with matching red basketball.

And that is not even it. The collection also includes accessories like a metal lunchbox, plastic lunch trays, a pack of styrofoam cups, a life-size cardboard cutout of the of Travis Scott himself, a selection of rugs for your house, and a giant Chicken McNugget body pillow.

The McDonald’s x Cactus Jack collection is currently on sale for a limited time while supplies last, on Travis Scott’s webstore. Note that most items will only ship in 4-12 weeks from now.
