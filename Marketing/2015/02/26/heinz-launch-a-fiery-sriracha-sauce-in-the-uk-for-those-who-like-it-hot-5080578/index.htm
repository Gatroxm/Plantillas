Heinz launch a fiery sriracha sauce in the UK – for those who like it HOT

Siam GoorwichThursday 26 Feb 2015 2:04 pm
Share this article via facebookShare this article via twitterShare this article via messenger
Share this with
Share this article via emailShare this article via flipboardCopy link

Ketchup, your time is up.

If you were left grief stricken by the news that Heinzs new sriracha spiked ketchup was only available in the USA (a travesty!), then we have some good news for you.

Heinz have seen sense and decided to launch their very own sriracha here in the UK. Yay!

And unlike the US version, the UK sauce is pure, unadulterated chilli joy.

Heinzs fiery sriracha is available from your local Asda, like, now, and other supermarkets later in the year.

As the Spice Girls would say, its time to spice up your life!
