McDonald’s Travis Scott meal: What’s in the cactus jack burger and can you get it in the UK?

Jack SlaterWednesday 9 Sep 2020 3:03 pm
Share this article via facebookShare this article via twitterShare this article via messenger
Share this with
Share this article via emailShare this article via flipboardCopy link

2020 has been weird, we can agree on that, but did anyone predict Sicko Mode rapper Travis Scott dropping a collaboration with Ronald McDonald?

Ron hasn’t swapped clownery for music yet – 28-year-old Travis Scott has teamed up with McDonald’s for the first celebrity meal since 1992, when they featured a Michael Jordan special.

Alongside the special meal, Travis Scott has launched a range of clothes in collab with McDonald’s. It is part of a month-long partnership between the restaurant chain and the Grammy-nominated artist’s record company, Cactus Jack.  

What is the Travis Scott Meal and can you get it in the UK?

What is the Travis Scott meal at McDonald’s?

Advertisement
Advertisement

The Travis Scott Meal contains three of Travis Scott’s favourite items from the menu for just $6 (£4.65).

They are a Quarter Pounder with Cheese with Travis’ go-to toppings: bacon and lettuce, plus medium fries with BBQ sauce and a Sprite.

It’s all a part of a special McDonald’s is running to boost their leeway with Millennial and Gen Z consumers, according to a report from Business Insider’s Kate Taylor.

 Can you get the Travis Scott Meal in the UK?

It looks as if this is an exclusive for fans in the United States.

Though, you could just order each of the items from the menu… It won’t be called the Travis Scott Meal, but you could pretend.

McDonald’s in the UK has just launched the Triple Cheeseburger though, so it’s not all bad news.
